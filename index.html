Beacon

Find the safest route with Beacon. Enter your start and end locations then filter crimes by type, time of day, and closeness to your path. Total crime counts and individual crime details available. Crime data available for Seattle only.

Market Tracker

Market Tracker enables users to simulate playing the stock market — lookup, view, and visualize stocks data, as well as research financial news. Log in or sign up to build a Portfolio by "Watching" and "Buying" stocks. Save a snapshot of their current market value, then compare later to see your simulated gains (or losses).

Travel Easy

Travel Easy aims to simplify comparing weather and flights for multiple cities. View forecasts and historical weather data, and log in or sign up to save a home city and as many destinations as desired.

Taste Saver

General Assembly Hackathon — "Making life better"

Get recipes from all your favorite sources in one place. Taste Saver enables users to look up and save recipes from a wide variety of sources. Search for recipes, ingredients, or dietary needs.
